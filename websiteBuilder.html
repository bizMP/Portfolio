Questions
FAQ

Do I need coding knowledge?

+

Absolutely not, coding is done in the background while you edit your site. Everything is in a drag and drop model.

Can I export clean HTML/CSS?

+

No, unfortunately you can not export HTML and CSS. The editor is meant as a preview tool. You can however get the site you built made specifically for you by pressing the "SEND" button in the editor.

Can I modify components?

+

Inside the editor components can not be directly customized. But by pressing the "SEND" button you can reach out to me and change whichever components you want.

Is it responsive?

+

Yes, you have a demo preview constantly in the middle of your editor. By clicking the "LIVE PREVIEW" button you can view it in your browser full screen and play with it.
